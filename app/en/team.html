The GDG Nantes TEAM

The Google Developers Groups (GDGs) are groups of people who are interested in Google created Platforms and technologies: Android, Chrome, Google Apps, GWT, AppEngine... These groups who share a common ideas can be found all around the world.

The objective of GDG Nantes is to organise events reguarly:

  • Google Technology Conferences and others on the Web, Cloud, Mobile.
  • Informal theme-based evening sessions around an Aperitif to facilitate meetings of similar minds and thoughts
  • Competitions (Hackathon, ...)

The Volunteers of DevFest

Each year, Epitech Nantes students volunteer to help the GDG team to set up the venue, the organization, the welcome, logistics and preparation of the event. We would like to thank them for their enthusiastic help!

Nantes Tech

The city of Nantes is increasingly becoming a popular centre for new technology with regularly organised events, conferences, exchanges and testing, proposed in a friendly environment.

These events put in place by new Web technology, cloud and Connected Objects’ aficionados are destined towards enthusiasts in the field. They also aim at improving the technological development in the Western regions of France, to participate in local professional dynamism, and to make Nantes a nationally recognized competence center in the field of new technologies.

Nantes is recognised and certified as among the premier cities in the French Tech; it is one of the most dynamic ecosystems of France in terms of web and digital innovation.
